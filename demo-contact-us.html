HOW SPONSORING A PET WORKS...

The SPCA is always in need of foster homes for the animals in our care! This could mean caring for a kitten with an upper respiratory infection, giving TLC to a dog with a broken leg, giving a long-time resident a break from shelter life, or simply providing a temporary home for an animal that needs time to grow. The SPCA provides all the food, supplies and medication needed. All we ask in return is that the animals are loved and nurtured while in your care. We do require that all of our foster parents be at least 21 years of age.
